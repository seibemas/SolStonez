About Me

My name is Mason, and I am the man behind the mirror that is creating these meaningful wrapped and weaved works of art!

My journey began when I was in my teens and I developed an interest for holistic health, stones, meditation, and the energy of our Human bodies! I started collecting, researching, and using crystals when I was 16. I was fascinated by their looks, and the subtle energy you can feel coming from them!

As time went on, I learned more about the Human energy field, also know as an aura, and how they are affected by stones especially.

When I turned 24 I bought my first wire wrapping tool kit, and started doing single wire wraps around lovely stone specimens. As I worked, and as I saw other peoples work, I realized that the stones could look even more physically beautiful, and feel energetically stronger as I got better at wrapping and weaving wire around them. Which leads us to this day, I am now 26 and have helped find the right stone, and wrap, for 100's of people!

Hopefully through this website I can help as many people as possible learn more about the effect stones have on our aura, and also help individuals find a little more harmony in their lives. I am not a doctor, and am not here to give medical advice, however, I have been helped by the stones around me, and wish the same for others. Hopefully together we can find the right stone, and wrap, for you!
