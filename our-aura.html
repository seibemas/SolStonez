Aura's and You

Our auras are made of an infinite number of layers, the seven layers displayed before you today are just the seven lower layers. Each layer encompasses all the layers below it, and each layer is related to a specific aspect of a person's being, and energy body.

  1. etheric - physical body;
    This layer is the closest to our physical bodies, it is there to sustain our bodies, and connect them to the higher levels of our aura. This layer of our aura exists approximately four inches away from the physical body, and is connected to the Muladhara, root chakra, considered to be the basis of Existence.

  2. emotional - emotions;
    This layer is directly outside the etheric, extending about seven inches from the physical body. It is connected to the Svadhisthana, sacral chakra, and it holds all our emotions, feelings, sexuality, and sensitivities.

  3. mental - thinking;
    This layer is directly outside the emotional layer, extending ten inches from the physical body. This layer contains our mental thought processes such as, disciplines, judgements, rules and regulations. It is connected to the Manipura, the naval chakra, where we derive our energy, will power, and sense of achievement, which radiates as prana (energy) throughout the entire human body.

  4. astral - I-thou emotions and desires;
    This layer extends about one foot from our physical bodies. It is considered to be the bridge between the lower vibrations, physical, and the higher vibrations, spiritual. It is connected to the Anahata, heart chakra, where our desires and emotions dance together to create a unique you.

  5. etheric template - higher will;
    This layer extends up to two feet from the physical body. It is shaped like the physical body, and acts as a blueprint for your body and soul. This layer is connected to the Vishuddha, throat chakra, our center for expression.

  6. celestial - higher feelings;
    This layer extends up to two and a half feet from the physical body, and is where your spiritual connection begins, and the process of enlightenment ensues. It is connected with the Ajna, third eye chakra, where our intuition is derived from.

  7. ketheric - higher concepts.
    This layer can extend up to three feet from the physical body. It is considered the spiritual layer, protecting all the other layers as it vibrates at the highest frequency of all seven. Connected to the Sahasrara, crown chakra, it relates to pure consciousness, enabling all other layers to exist.
Source: Daniel J. Benor, M.D. Healing Research, Volume II, How Can I Heal What Hurts? Wholistic Healing and Bioenergies. Medford, NJ. Wholistic Healing Publications, © Copyright 2005.
